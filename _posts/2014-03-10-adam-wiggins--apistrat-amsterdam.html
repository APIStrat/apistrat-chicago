We are excited to announce Adam Wiggins as a keynote speaker for our upcoming Apistrat conference, 'Scaling in the API Economy.' Adam Wiggins is a hacker and entrepreneur, best known for cofounding Heroku, and will be at the APIStrat Amsterdam talking about home automation and the role of APIs in a hackable worldon Friday, March 28 in the afternoon. This will be the second part of his talk 'Hackability: The Future of Programming in a Post-PC World', and he will be delivering it for the fist time at APIStrat Amsterdam.
